# DR Laws Explainer Site — Project Spec

Opened 2026-06-07. Side project. Status: MVP LIVE (Kelvin approved override 2026-06-07).
LIVE URL: https://kevthetech143.github.io/leyes-rd/
Repo: https://github.com/Kevthetech143/leyes-rd (account Kevthetech143, public)
8 sectors of sample laws + all 32 provinces (leaders are honest placeholders).
Data is sample/placeholder only; real congress feed + voting records not yet wired.

## Goal
Free public website that makes Dominican Republic lawmaking understandable to adults
with low formal education. "Built like it's for kids, used by adults."

## Why
No site exists that shows DR laws in plain language, grouped by meaning, with voting
records and a province map. Official congress trackers (Senate SIL, Chamber SIL) are
Spanish, clunky, lawyer-facing, and do NOT publish clean per-legislator vote records.

## Hard requirements (from Kelvin)
- Free static hosting via GitHub Pages.
- No login to use.
- Language: Spanish, short, direct, kid-friendly reading level.
- Laws GROUPED BY SECTOR (health, jobs, crime, etc.) — NEVER displayed by title or ID number.
- Each law shows: what it is, and WHY it was brought up.
- Expandable UI: click a law to see details, including who voted (if data exists).
- Province/sector map: click a region -> see leadership -> each leader's title, full
  profile, and voting record.
- Fun, kid-friendly look, adult use.

## Open feasibility risks (verify before promising)
1. Per-legislator voting records are NOT cleanly published by DR congress. Must confirm
   what vote data can actually be pulled before committing to that feature.
2. "Real-time" on static hosting = scheduled job that scrapes congress sites and rebuilds.
3. Plain-language + sector grouping of raw bills likely needs an AI summarization step.

## Closest existing sources
- Senate Legislative Initiatives (SIL): https://www.senadord.gob.do/iniciativas-legislativa/
- Chamber of Deputies SIL: https://www.camaradediputados.gob.do/
